Introduction

I'm a software developer and graduate student at the University of Pittsburgh. I have a wide range of interests across the field and throughout the technology stack. I am currently learning more about machine learning, distributed systems, and mobile application development.

Current Projects

A mobile app with the goal of reducing food waste by feeding leftovers to hungry college students. We are building a recommender system to connect students to the food events they're most interested in attending.
